WHO WE ARE

Ramen Ippudo Vietnam
Japanese Restaurant

In 1985, Ippudo began as a humble ramen shop in Hakata, Japan, with just ten counter seats. Led by Ramen King Shigemi Kawahara, we set out to share the joy of Japanese ramen with the world.

Today, we’ve expanded to over 200 stores across 16 countries worldwide. Timeless flavors, heartfelt hospitality, and a balance of tradition and innovation are at the heart of every bowl we serve.
